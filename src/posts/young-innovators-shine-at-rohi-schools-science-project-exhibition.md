At Rohi Group of Schools, creativity and innovation were on full display as our students proudly presented their Science Project Exhibition.

The event showcased a wide range of impressive projects built entirely from the students’ creativity, research, and teamwork. From working volcano models and simple electric circuits to water purification systems and environmental awareness prototypes, the exhibition demonstrated practical learning at its best.

Parents, teachers, and invited guests were amazed at the confidence, originality, and problem-solving skills displayed by our young scientists.

The exhibition reinforced our commitment to hands-on learning — where students are encouraged not just to memorize concepts, but to apply knowledge in real-life situations.

At Rohi Schools, we don’t just teach science — we inspire innovation.
