Rohi Group of Schools wishes to inform all parents, guardians, and students that the current academic term will officially come to an end on April 2nd, marking the beginning of the end-of-term vacation. We appreciate the dedication, hard work, and commitment shown by our students throughout the term, as well as the continuous support from parents and guardians. Students are encouraged to make good use of the holiday period by engaging in productive activities, revising their academic work, and maintaining good conduct at all times. Please note that the resumption date for the next term will be communicated in due course. Parents and guardians are advised to stay connected through our official communication channels for updates. We wish all our students a safe, enjoyable, and refreshing holiday. Rohi Group of Schools Raising principled leaders through sound education.
