What a colorful and exciting day it was at Rohi Group of Schools as we celebrated our annual Cultural Day 2026! 🎉

The event was filled with beautiful displays of tradition, creativity, and unity. Our students proudly showcased their diverse cultural backgrounds through dance, drama, music, and traditional cuisine — reminding us of the beauty and strength in our diversity. 🌍✨

Parents, teachers, and guests were thrilled to see how confidently the children represented various ethnic groups with pride and joy. It was more than a day of celebration — it was a reminder that at Rohi, we raise students who value culture, respect diversity, and celebrate togetherness.

We’re proud of every student who participated and grateful to everyone who made the day a success. 💚
